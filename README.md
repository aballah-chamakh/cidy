
## Cidy
Cidy is a mobile app designed for teachers who give private lessons, helping them manage their students efficiently. The app offers features such as:

- A dashboard to view key business KPIs, including:
  - The number of students enrolled in each subject
  - The total amount paid by students per subject
  - The unpaid amounts for students per subject
- A weekly calendar to manage their schedule
- The ability to organize students into groups
- Attendance, absence, and payment tracking for each student

Cidy aims to simplify administrative tasks for tutors and improve the overall management of their private lessons.
