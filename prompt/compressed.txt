The "Cidy" app is a mobile application designed for government teachers in Tunisia who run private classes. It provides the following features:

- **Teacher Features:**
  - Track student attendance and payments.
  - Receive notifications for due payments.
  - View revenue and due payments.
  - Manage weekly schedules.
  - Send payment notifications to students and parents.

- **Parent Features:**
  - View their child's attendance and payments.
  - Prevent students from misleading parents about payments or attendance.

- **Student Features:**
  - View their own attendance and payments.

The app includes three profiles: Teacher, Student, and Parent, with common login and registration screens.

### Common Screens:
1. **Login Screen:**
   - Email and password inputs with validation.
   - Login button routes to the appropriate dashboard.
   - Link to the registration screen.
2. **Register Screen:**
   - Profile type selection (Teacher, Student, Parent).
   - Inputs for name, email, phone, and password with validation.
   - Register button creates an account and routes to the dashboard.

### Teacher Profile:
- **App Bar:** Includes a sidebar toggle, screen title, and notification bell.
- **Sidebar Navigation:**
  - Profile, Dashboard, Week Schedule, Groups, Students, Prices, Student Requests, Parent Requests, Notifications, and Account.
- **Screens:**
  1. Dashboard: KPIs for paid/unpaid amounts, filters by level, section, and subject.
  2. Week Schedule: Weekly calendar with draggable group cards.
  3. Groups: List of groups with search, filters, and group details.
  4. Students: List of students with filters and student details.
  5. Prices: Manage levels, sections, and subjects.
  6. Student Requests: Accept or decline student requests.
  7. Parent Requests: Accept or decline parent requests.
  8. Notifications: List of notifications with details.
  9. Account: Update profile and change password.

### Student Profile:
- **App Bar:** Similar to the teacher profile.
- **Sidebar Navigation:** Profile, Teachers, Subjects, Parents, and Notifications.
- **Screens:**
  1. Teachers: List of teachers with reenroll option.
  2. Subjects: List of subjects with subject details.
  3. Parents: List of accepted parents.
  4. Notifications: List of notifications with details.

### Parent Profile:
- **App Bar:** Similar to the teacher profile.
- **Sidebar Navigation:** Profile, Teachers, Sons, and Notifications.
- **Screens:**
  1. Teachers: List of teachers with parenting request option.
  2. Sons: List of sons with details and add son option.
  3. Notifications: List of notifications with details.

### Technical Requirements:
- Built with Kotlin Multiplatform Mobile (KMM).
- Backend: Django Rest Framework (DRF) with PostgreSQL.
- Responsive design with light and dark themes.
